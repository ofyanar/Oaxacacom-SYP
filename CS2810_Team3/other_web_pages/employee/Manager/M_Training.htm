Manager Training



Responsibilities

  • Recruiting, training and supervising staff - giving the staff a brief description on what to do and their responsibility.
  • Agreeing and managing budgets - Making sure that we have enough funds for food supply for the day.
  • Planning menus - checking which food stock is available and updating the menu according to that.
  • Ensuring compliance with licensing, hygiene and health and safety legislation/guidelines.
  • Promoting and marketing the business - making sure the website is working at all time.
  • Overseeing stock levels - making sure that the stock levels are delivered on time
  • Ordering supplies
  • Producing staff rotas - giving a brief explanation for the job responsibility for the day.
  • Handling customer enquiries and complaints.
  • Taking reservations - making the free waiters take in customer that haven't booked a table and letting them know how long will it take for their turn.
  • Greeting and advising customers - making sure that the waiters are greetinga and advising customers when they order.
  • Problem solving.
  • Preparing and presenting staffing/sales reports.
  • Keeping statistical and financial records.
  • Assessing and improving profitability.
  • Setting targets.
  • Making improvements to the running of the business and developing the restaurant.

Customer services

  • Greeting and advising customers.
  • Delivering F&B service to satisfy customer expectations.
  • Fostering positive customer relations.
  • Dealing with customer inquiries and complaints.

Human resources

  • Recruiting, training and supervising staff.
  • Managing the staff rota.
  • Conducting performance appraisals and setting targets.
  • Optimizing workflow and productivity.

Supply chain, procurement and culinary edge

  • Researching dishes and planning menus.
  • Keeping abreast of inventory and ordering supplies.
  • Monitoring product quality and handling vendors.

Compliance, health and safety

  • Ensuring compliance with licensing, health and safety regulations
  • Establishing and maintaining policies and protocols

How to navigate through the website

  • Login in first.
  • After logging in, you will be able access the manager page.
  • In the manager page, you will be given 6 buttons/category for the managing your job. The buttons are "Your Training", "Brach status", "Revenue", "Stocks", "Manage Account" and "Logout".
  • Once you click the "Your Training" button, you will be taken to the new page which displays you responsibilities as a manager and how use the website for managing the restaurent.
  • Once you click the "Brach status" button, you will be taken to the new page which displays status of the branch, so you will be allowed to change the status of the branch to be open or close.
  • Once you click the "Revenue" button, you will be taken to the new page which displays the revenue generated for this month.
  • Once you click the "Stocks" button, you will be taken to the new page which displays current menu stock, the current menu stocks will be displayed by a table in which you can view the item name and the item quantity, if the item quality is low for specific item you can press the restock button and the item quantity will update.
  • Once you click the "Manage Account" button, you will be taken to the new page which displays three different buttons which are "add account", "edit account", "delete account". For add account you can just make new employee account and assign a position to it. For edit account, you can change the employee password and the position. Lastly for delete account you can just select a specific employee ID and username to delete an existing account
  • Once you click the "Logout" button, you will be logged out the website.
