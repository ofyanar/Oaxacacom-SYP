About Us


Oaxaca is a restaurant group that has been slowly growing its London and UK presence over the last 10 years and now they have 25 restaurants. When they started in 2007 their competitive advantage was their menu, a unique style of Mexican street food and their table service.

We’re passionate about providing unique style of Mexican street food to customers. This relentless focus has helped us grow in UK.

Since opening the first Oaxaaca’s store in the UK in 2007, we now have 25 stores across the UK and more than "200" team members. This year, we are looking to expand Oaxaca's restaurant even more.

We’re committed to offering choice, and with different types of combinations on our menu, customers have plenty of options. Our Mexican street food are made with great quality sourced ingredients, such as Tomatillos, nopal, beans, and Shredded beef and chicken etc.

There are two ways to dine with us. One is off the menu which offers small plates, burrito's, nachos, tacos, quesadillas, platitos and desserts like Churros, drinks like mexican Hot Chocolate, beer etc. In addition to our printed menu, guests can also order directly online which is user friendly and quick to use. These nightly specials will be described tableside/online and range in price from $4-$12.
